Role Descriptions

Defense

Field Coach - Coach players before/during after the play. Remind players how many outs, where's the play, how many runners, correct positioning. Be a coach of the game!

Ball Collector - Keep the field clean! You are responsible for collecting and bagging all foul/passed balls. Remind your catcher each inning to roll you any caught balls.

RapidBullpen Coach - EXTREMELY important to make both players pitch at the same cadence and in unison. This is controlled by verbal cues with the 3-Step Method. Please refer to the RapidBullpen Guide for further instruction.

Offense

Pitching Machine Operator - You control the pace of play! Be quick, and be consistent. Present baseball to batter by holding overhead then smoothly feed ball into chute. If balls are damp, use towel to quickly dry before loading. Machine WON'T fire damp/wet baseballs. No more than 3 pitches!

First Base Coach - Encourage players to run hard through first base and turn off into foul territory. Remind runners how many outs. Help with clearing the bases every time the defense records three outs.

Dugout / Lineup Coordinator - Make sure players are ready to bat. Always have your on-deck and in-the-hole batters ready to go. Encourage them to hustle to home plate!
